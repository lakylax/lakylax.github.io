Sathi — AI Mental Health Companion

Project Overview

I developed the AI integration for Sathi, a Flutter-based wellbeing app designed for Nepalese students living abroad. Built during the Nepal-US Hackathon 2026, Sathi combines voice reflection, photo sharing, weekly mental health check-ins, a private social feed, and an Android home-screen widget into one supportive experience.

My role focused on integrating Google's Gemini API for AI-powered mood analysis, voice transcription feedback, and personalized mental health suggestions.

My Role: AI Integration (Gemini API)

As the AI Integration lead, I was responsible for:

  • Connecting the Python transcription backend with Google Gemini API
  • Building the mood analysis pipeline that processes voice journals
  • Generating supportive, culturally-aware feedback from transcribed audio
  • Creating the insight scoring system that combines weekly check-in data with voice sentiment signals
  • Designing prompts that produce Flutter-ready payloads including mood, energy levels, summaries, and suggestions

Key Features I Enabled

Voice Journal AI Feedback

Users record voice journals; the backend transcribes with Whisper, then Gemini analyzes the content to provide: - Mood classification and energy level assessment - Supportive, non-judgmental summary of what was expressed - Culturally-aware suggestions for South Asian users - Safety detection for crisis situations

Weekly Wellbeing Insights

The Insights tab combines structured weekly check-in responses with recent voice journal sentiment signals to produce a composite mental health score — powered by my Gemini integration.

Mobile Widget Intelligence

The home-screen widget surfaces a quick wellbeing snapshot with: - Mood keywords extracted from recent activity - A brief support suggestion - Photo context from shared posts

Key Takeaways

  • Gemini API excels at generating empathetic, culturally-sensitive mental health responses when properly prompted
  • Voice-to-insight pipelines require careful handling of transcription errors before sentiment analysis
  • Safety/crisis detection must be a first-class concern in any mental health AI application
  • Flutter's cross-platform capability allowed rapid prototyping across mobile and web
